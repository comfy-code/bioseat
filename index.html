ASSURANCE IN DRIVERLESS CARS

Added safety for all on the road.

Core Features

Health Tracking

Bioseat actively monitors your health through biometric scans activated by the seat.

Cyber Security

A included VPN network protects vehice's online network from cyber attacks/hacking.

3rd Party App Integration

Many apps like Apple's health app can be used to share health data with bioseat.

Health Alerts

Recieve health alerts such as irregular Rhythm or low heart rate.

Safe 2 Drive

Each time bioseat is activated by the driver bioseat will instantly determine whether it is safe for the driver to operate the vehicle based on tracked biometrics.

Alert Authorities

If health is determined to be detrimental to operate a vehicle, authorities will be notified & dispatched to you.

How It Works

1

Upload Personal Info

Provide the nescessary health information to give bioseat an initial analysis to observe your current health.

2

Install bioseat

Once you've downloaded the app and uploaded the personal info you are ready to install the physical seat and enjoy a comfortable safe drive.

3

Hit the road: SAFE 2 DRIVE

Upon every drive with bioseat you will be alerted by the app with a notification "Safe 2 Drive" based on your health tracked through bioseats biometrics scanning.
